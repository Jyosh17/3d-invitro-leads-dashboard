This project is a **Streamlit dashboard** designed to identify, rank, and prioritize potential collaborators or clients for 3D in-vitro models. It simulates a lead generation and scoring system for biotech and pharmaceutical research teams.

---

## **Features**

- Identify top leads based on:
  - Role fit (e.g., Director of Toxicology, Head of Preclinical Safety)
  - Company intent (funding stage, budget availability)
  - Technographic adoption (existing similar technologies)
  - Location (on-site vs remote)
  - Scientific activity (recent publications)
- Dynamic ranking with **probability score** (0-100)
- Interactive table with columns:
  - `Rank | Probability | Name | Company | Location | HQ | WorkMode | Email`
- CSV export functionality
- Colorful, interactive UI with relevant images
- Easy filtering by location or field
